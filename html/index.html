Election protection hotline:

Have a question about the voting process in your state or experiencing difficulties voting in person? Call the free Election Protection hotline to be connected to an expert who can support you.

English: 1-866 OUR VOTE

Spanish: 1-888-VE-Y-VOTA

Arabic: 1-844-YALLA-US

For Bengali, Cantonese, Hindi, Urdu, Korean, Mandarin, Tagalog, or Vietnamese: 1-888-274-8683
