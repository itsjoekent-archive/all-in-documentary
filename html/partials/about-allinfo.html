All In For Voting
Bus Tour

To launch the documentary film All In: The Fight for Democracy, the #AllInForVoting campaign hit the road to safely educate, mobilize and register voters. The film was shown at socially distanced outdoor pop-up screenings for as many people as we could fit. A fleet of five buses toured across 10 states with high levels of voter restrictions, setting up temporary voting action stations in over 50 locations.

The best remedy to combat voter suppression is voter turnout!
Hope we caught you on the road!
