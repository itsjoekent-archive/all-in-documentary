All In For Voting
Bus Tour

To launch the documentary film All In: The Fight for Democracy, the #AllInForVoting campaign is hitting the road to safely educate, mobilize and register voters. The film will be shown at socially distanced outdoor pop-up screenings for as many people as we can. A fleet of five buses will tour across 10 states with high levels of voter restrictions, setting up temporary voting action stations in over 50 locations.

Guests will be able to:

  • - Register to vote
  • - Check their registration
  • - Find their local polling place
  • - Request a mail-in ballot
  • - Volunteer to be a poll worker

The best remedy to combat voter suppression is voter turnout!
Hope to catch you on the road!
